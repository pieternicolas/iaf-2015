Indonesian Arts Festival


The Indonesian Arts Festival (IAF) is an established event held biennially by the Indonesian Students’ Association in Singapore (PPI Singapura) and is fully supported by the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia for Singapore since 2003. IAF aims to be a platform in which arts professionals and arts enthusiasts could interact together as a community. IAF celebrates the unity in diversity in Singapore as a cosmopolitan and multi-racial society by raising the awareness for Indonesian traditional arts among the Singaporean society as part of the South East Asian heritage.

In this year’s edition, IAF will be exploring how to promote a trademark of Indonesian culture, namely the Wayang Wong by combining the elegance of the traditional form of art with the exuberance of pop music and dance.

The Dancing Shadows:
A Festival


To further promote our campaign, IAF will hold festivals in the form of flashmob from December 2014 to January 2015 in random public places in Singapore. The IAF crews will showcase a 15-minute performance of Wayang infused with contemporary songs and dance which will leave you in awe for more.

Chest of Shadows: A Musical


This year’s IAF will be concluded with a musical, taking the essence of Wayang Wong performance and recreate a new definition with the twist of modern digital technology, pop and contemporary art and tunes that will keep you tapping. The musical will be held on 14-15 February 2015 at Victoria Theatre, Singapore.
